A meaningful gift that lasts generations.

Imagine giving a gift that doesn’t bring joy for just a moment, but creates a lasting impact for future generations. Through FNPW’s Landscape Resilience program, you can gift a tree that helps restore Australia’s natural landscapes and supports vital ecosystems.

Born from the devastation of the 2019/2020 Black Summer bushfires, our program rebuilds and reconnects Australia’s ecosystems through 30+ community-based nurseries across four states, supporting our commitment to the Global Biodiversity Framework pledge to restore 30% of degraded ecosystems by 2030.

How it works

From seed to forest, in three steps.

01

Grown from seed

Our environmental experts carefully select native species suited to each local ecosystem. Trees are grown from seed in 30+ community nurseries across Australia.

02

Planted by volunteers

Trained local volunteers plant your trees in fire- and flood-affected landscapes, where they have the best chance to survive and thrive.

03

Monitored for life

Once planted, sites are monitored to ensure tree survival, ecosystem recovery, and the return of native wildlife to restored Country.

Wollondilly Community Nursery

Where each tree begins its journey.

The Wollondilly Community Nursery has been an example of environmental hope for over two decades, drawing volunteers and residents into its mission to create a thriving habitat for koalas in the region.

“Each tree we propagate is a commitment to the land, to the wildlife and to the generations who’ll inherit this Country.”

Your gift directly funds the seed collection, propagation, and care that turns a single native seed into a tree ready for planting.

Questions

Frequently asked

When will my trees be planted?

Trees are planted seasonally based on local rainfall and species requirements. Most plantings occur in autumn and spring across our 30+ community nursery sites. You’ll receive updates on the progress of your gift.

When will my recipient get their certificate?

Digital certificates are issued the next business day after your donation. You can specify a future delivery date during checkout if you’d like to time it with a special occasion.

Is my donation tax deductible?

Yes. FNPW is a registered Australian charity (ABN 51 248 905 949) with Deductible Gift Recipient status. Donations of $2 or more are tax deductible in Australia.

What species are planted?

Only 100% native species, carefully chosen by our environmental experts to match the ecology of each planting site. This ensures the trees thrive and rebuild functioning ecosystems, not just monocultures.

Can I order trees for my company or event?

Absolutely. For corporate orders, memorial plantings, or quantities over 100 trees, please contact our partnerships team at hello@fnpw.org.au for a tailored arrangement.
