What we do

Three pillars. One mission.

From the wetlands of the Murray Mouth to the edge of Boodjamulla, our work protects intact landscapes, brings species back, and heals what’s been damaged.

Pillar 01

Growing Parks

Protecting high-value land is one of the most effective ways to halt nature’s decline. We secure landscapes of high biodiversity value into permanent protection.

29,479 ha added to NP status
Pillar 02

Saving Species

Science-led recovery programs that address key threats and strengthen the long-term survival of Australia’s most vulnerable wildlife.

18,582 wombat mange treatments
Pillar 03

Healing Our Land

Through large-scale restoration, community nurseries and climate-adaptive planting, we help degraded ecosystems recover and reconnect.

647 ha under restoration in 2025
