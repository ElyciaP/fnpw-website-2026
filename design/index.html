Since 1970

Fifty-five years of looking after the wild.

It started with a single gift and a simple idea: that Australia’s national parks and wildlife deserve a foundation of their own. Since then we have helped grow the protected estate, fund species recovery from the genome to the nest box, and heal country alongside its Traditional Custodians.

We are independent, apolitical and stubborn about outcomes. The next fifty-five years start with you.
