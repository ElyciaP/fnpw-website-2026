Frequently asked questions.

Quick answers about donations, receipts, volunteering and more.

Is my donation tax deductible?

Yes. FNPW is endorsed as a Deductible Gift Recipient, so donations of $2 or more are tax deductible. We email receipts automatically.

Where does my money go?

Across our three pillars: growing national parks, saving species and healing the land. See how your contributions help.

Can my company volunteer with you?

Yes, we run corporate planting and restoration days in most capital cities. See corporate volunteering.

How do I leave a gift in my Will?

Our bequests page explains the process and the wording your solicitor needs. You can also call us for a confidential chat on 1800 898 626.

How do I gift a tree?

Through our Gift a Tree program. We plant a native tree and send a certificate to your recipient.

How do I contact FNPW?

Email us via the contact page or call 1800 898 626. Post: GPO Box 2666, Sydney NSW 2001.
